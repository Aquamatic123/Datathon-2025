IMPORTANT:
==========================================================

1. Lambda execution role provides AWS credentials automatically
2. Your local .env.local STILL needs APP_ACCESS_KEY_ID and 
   APP_SECRET_ACCESS_KEY for development
3. On Amplify, the Lambda role must have permissions for:
   - sagemaker:InvokeEndpoint
   - dsql:DbConnectAdmin

==========================================================
HOW TO ADD THESE IN AMPLIFY CONSOLE:
==========================================================

1. Go to AWS Amplify Console
2. Click on your app
3. Click "App settings" → "Environment variables"
4. Click "Manage variables"
5. Add the 4 variables above
6. Click "Save"
7. Redeploy your app
