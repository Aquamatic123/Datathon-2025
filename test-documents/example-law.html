Digital Privacy Protection Act 2024

Jurisdiction

United States

Status

Active

Sector

Technology

Published Date

March 20, 2024

Summary

This act establishes comprehensive data privacy protections for consumers in the digital age. It requires technology companies to obtain explicit consent before collecting personal data, provides consumers with the right to access and delete their data, and imposes significant penalties for data breaches.

The law has significant implications for major technology companies, particularly those involved in social media, data analytics, and cloud computing. Companies must implement new compliance measures within 180 days of enactment.
