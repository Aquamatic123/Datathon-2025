Financial Services Reform Act 2024

Jurisdiction: European Union
Status: Pending
Sector: Finance
Published: April 1, 2024

Summary:
This comprehensive financial reform act introduces stricter capital requirements
for banks and financial institutions operating in the European Union. The legislation
aims to prevent future financial crises by ensuring banks maintain higher capital
reserves and implement more robust risk management practices.

Key Provisions:
- Increased capital adequacy ratios
- Enhanced stress testing requirements
- Stricter lending standards
- Improved consumer protections
- Greater transparency in financial reporting

Impact Assessment:
The act is expected to have a moderate to high impact on the financial sector,
particularly affecting large multinational banks and investment firms. Estimated
market impact: 7/10 with medium confidence due to implementation timeline uncertainties.

Affected Entities:
Major banks, investment firms, insurance companies, and financial technology companies
operating in EU markets will need to comply with new regulations within 24 months.
